Privacy Policy

HomeOps respects your privacy. This app is designed to work fully offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

HomeOps does not collect any personal or usage data. All information you enter is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

Accounts & Tracking

HomeOps does not require accounts, does not use analytics, and does not include tracking or third-party SDKs.

Notifications

Any notifications generated by HomeOps are created and delivered locally on your device. No server communication is involved.
